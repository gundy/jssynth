JSSynth Hello World

Haven't you heard? Raw, un-styled web pages are back in vogue.

This one provides a simple demonstration of jssynth, and it's ability to play Amiga MOD files, while also playing sound effects, or other generated noises.

To use the demo, first click "Initialise Audio", then you can use the "Start"/"Stop" buttons to start/stop music playback by enabling/disabling the browser's web audio callbacks.
